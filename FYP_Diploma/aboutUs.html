Give & Gather is a forward-thinking humanitarian project with its headquarters in Malaysia that aims to further religious development, vital medical assistance, and education. Our goal is to improve society by meeting pressing needs and promoting inclusivity, teamwork, and collaboration. Our web platform aims to improve user experience, expedite the donation procedure, and enable charity giving for everyone.
